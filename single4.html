Whatsapp will soon let you use the same account on multiple devices

Sriyansa Mohanty   Mar 3, 2020

WhatsApp has been able to hit a global audience and crack through 2 billion monthly users despite a significant handicap: different apps with the same account are not enabled by the program. One phone number is tantamount to one phone or laptop, that’s always the equation.

A study published by WABetainfo says that support for various devices is something that the program doesn’t currently have. The latest WhatsApp beta version 2.20.110 will support several apps, as new references have been discovered, according to the article. Through this, the user will log in to the same account on various devices he or she has on the network. As a result, users can chat over multiple devices on the same account.

If this is valid, your WhatsApp account may soon be used on several devices, such as your cell phone and laptop. This is different than using WhatsApp on the web only for clarity. The new feature will allow users to search their accounts on multiple devices as part of the native WhatsApp messaging app.
